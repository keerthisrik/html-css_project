UI/UX Design

UI/UX Design involves creating user-centered and visually appealing digital products.

Subjects Covered:

  • User Research and Analysis
  • Wireframing and Prototyping
  • User Interface (UI) Design
  • User Experience (UX) Design
  • Interaction Design

Topics:

  • Design Thinking
  • Human-Computer Interaction (HCI)
  • Visual Design Principles
  • Usability Testing and Feedback
  • Design Systems and Style Guides

Career Paths:

  • UI/UX Designer
  • Product Designer
  • Interaction Designer
  • Visual Designer
  • Design Director

Skills Required:

  • Design tools (Sketch, Figma, Adobe XD)
  • Prototyping tools (InVision, (link unavailable))
  • User research and analysis methods
  • Visual design principles and typography
  • Communication and collaboration skills

Certifications:

  • Certified UI/UX Designer
  • Design Thinking Certification
  • UX Design Certification
